Recent Advancements

We have made significant Advancements. Here are some of our recent advancements:

  • Robotics: Our robotic systems can perform complex tasks, such as Real Human Interactions, demonstrating near human-level problem-solving capabilities.
  • Multi-Agent Systems: We have made progress in enhancing Robot Training using AiRParrot SDK
  • AI Safety: We are continuously improving the robustness and safety of AI systems, ensuring they will not spew false info. or cause harm
  • Research: Our research includes ensuring Robots can be compaions to human and follow values and ethical considerations, promoting trust and acceptance.
